Instructions

Hi there, here are the Instructions for how to play the game. It is a memory game where there will be some
sets of cards which are placed upside down. First is You will be given number of moves to play, A move
is considered when you flip 2 cards. You want to click the cards to flip it, after flipping two cards,
if the two cards are same, it will stay opened and your score will increase, number of moves will decrease and
if not the cards will flip back and you number of moves also decrease. The game will end when the number of moves is 0
And lastly there will be a timer for 1 minute and you want to complete the game within the time otherwise the game will end.
